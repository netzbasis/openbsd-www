OpenSMTPD Mailing Lists

Developers private list

Security-related issues may be sent to the private list opensmtpd-security@openbsd.org which is read only by the OpenSMTPD developers. It is not appropriate for general enquiries, see below for more appropriate places for those.

Other issues may be sent to the private list bugs@opensmtpd.org which is read only by the OpenSMTPD developers. It is not appropriate for general enquiries, see below for more appropriate places for those.

General OpenSMTPD user discussion list

General purpose discussions, issues and ideas may be discussed on our misc@opensmtpd.org list (Archive). The list is not moderated, however registration is required.

To register, simply send a mail to to misc+subscribe@opensmtpd.org.
To unregister, send a mail to misc+unsubscribe@opensmtpd.org.

At this time, the list is very low volume, feel free to introduce yourself ;-)
