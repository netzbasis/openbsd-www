OpenBSD Crypto 2000




June 16 - 20, 2000
(preceding the Usenix technical conference in San Diego)

Calgary, Alberta, Canada

Summary
A small cosey invitation-only conference in Calgary the week before Usenix. An ideal (non-USA) setting for developers to discuss, design, and implement new strategic developments which will lead to greater deployment of cryptographic and other related components in OpenBSD.

Wavelan, sparcstations running X, and various other network services will be provided, and inexpensive Hotel accommodation is nearby.
