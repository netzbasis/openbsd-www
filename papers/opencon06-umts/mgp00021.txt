
OpenBSD Hardware Support

GSM/GPRS/EDGE/UMTS hardware we currently support (these should work on i386, amd64, macppc, zaurus to some extend):

com(4)/pcom(4):

Siemens Connect2AIR CF (GPRS only)
Novatel Wireless Merlin U530 / U630
Sierra Wireless A550, A555 CDMA 1x, and A710, A750 modems
Sony Ericcson GC75, GC89

ubsa(4)/umsm(4)/ucom(4)/umodem(4):

Huawei Mobile Connect E618
Option Globetrotter 3G Fusion
Option Globetrotter 3G Quad
Sierra Wireless MC8755
Vodafone Mobile Connect 3G
lots and lots of cell phones attached via USB
