The work to open WLAN-chipsets - Some milestones
  • atu (Atmel 802.11b USB)
    The firmware got a free license
  • iwi/ipw (Intel PRO/Wireless "Centrino" 802.11a/b/g)
    The bad license remains! Included drivers without firmware and a note about peter.engelbrecht@intel.com (858- 391 1857) in ipw(4) and iwi(4)
  • (u)ral (Ralink 802.11a/b/g)
    Got a working driver for the SDR based on the Linux reference driver
  • rtw (Realtek 802.11b)
    Got the specs for the 8180L-chipset directly from Realtek
  • wi (Symbol)
    The firmware for the Spektrum24 got a free license
